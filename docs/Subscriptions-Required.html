Part 2 — Communicating With Users

Completely Free

Firebase is Google's service that delivers these instant notifications. It works 24/7 without any cost.

Why you cannot skip it: Without Firebase, donors would never know about blood requests unless they manually open the app and check. The entire real-time matching system depends on these notifications.

5
DLT Registration — Indian Government Requirement
Provider: Jio / Airtel / Vodafone DLT Portal  |  Mandated by TRAI
~₹500 — One-time + 7 days wait

What this is in simple words: India's telecom regulator (TRAI) passed a law that says any business sending SMS messages to customers must register themselves officially. This is to prevent spam and fraud. It's like getting a trade license before you can operate.

Why you cannot skip it: If you skip DLT registration, every OTP message your app tries to send will be blocked by Jio, Airtel, and Vodafone automatically. No user will be able to log in because they'll never receive their OTP code. This is a legal requirement — not optional.

Cost
~₹500 (processing fee)
Time to Approve
5–7 business days
What You Register
Your company name + SMS template
Where to Register
Jio / Airtel / Vodafone DLT portal
Start this first — before anything else. DLT approval takes 5–7 business days and blocks your entire launch if delayed. This should be the very first step.

MSG91 — SMS Service for OTP Login
Provider: MSG91  |  msg91.com
₹5,000–15,000/month (varies)

Why you cannot skip it: Your app uses phone number + OTP as the only way to log in (no passwords). Without an SMS service, users cannot log in at all. MSG91 is India's leading SMS provider with the best delivery rates and DLT compliance built in.

Part 3 — The Engine That Runs the App
8
Railway — App Server & Database
Provider: Railway  |  railway.app
₹5,800–8,300/month

What this is in simple words: Your app needs a powerful computer running 24 hours a day, 7 days a week — one that stores all user data (donor profiles, blood requests, hospital info) and responds instantly when anyone opens the app. Railway is like renting that computer online. You don't buy the computer; you just pay monthly to use it.

Railway handles 4 things for your app:

(a) API Server — The brain that processes all requests. When a user posts a blood request, this server finds matching donors nearby and notifies them.

(b) Worker — A background assistant that handles tasks like sending notifications, escalating requests to farther donors if no one responds nearby, and cleaning up expired requests.

(c) Database (PostgreSQL) — The filing cabinet where all permanent data lives: user accounts, blood requests, donation history, match records, etc.

(d) Cache (Redis) — A quick-access memory that makes the app respond faster. For example, it stores recent nearby requests so users don't wait long when they open the app.

Monthly Cost
~$70–100 (~₹5,800–8,300)
Paid How Often
Every month
Handles
Server + Database + Cache + Worker
Capacity
Easily handles 1 lakh users
Note: Railway's pricing is based on actual usage (like electricity — you pay for what you use). ₹5,800–8,300/month is an estimate for 1 lakh users. It may be lower in early months when usage is less.

Why you cannot skip it: Without a domain name, you cannot set up proper HTTPS security (the padlock that shows your connection is safe). You also cannot change your hosting provider later without breaking everything — a domain name gives you flexibility.

Important Notes Before You Start
⚡
Start DLT Registration Today
DLT registration with Jio/Airtel/Vodafone takes 5–7 business days for government approval. This is the one thing that cannot be rushed. Everything else can be set up in a day, but DLT blocks your entire launch if not done in advance.
📱
Apple Requires Annual Renewal
Apple charges $99 every year without exception. Set a calendar reminder 30 days before the renewal date. If payment fails and the account lapses, your app is removed from the App Store for all iPhone users.
💰
SMS Costs Depend on Usage
MSG91 charges per OTP sent. Costs go up if users log in frequently or if OTPs are resent multiple times. The ₹5,000–15,000 estimate assumes average login behavior. Keep the MSG91 wallet topped up — it works like a prepaid balance.
📈
Costs Will Stay Low Initially
In the first few months, when user numbers are growing gradually, Railway and SMS costs will be on the lower end of the estimates. The numbers above represent steady-state costs at 1 lakh active users.
